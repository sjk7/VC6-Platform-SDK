Set the Object Pooling property

This sequence configures COM+ to create the specified number of pooled components when the component is initially created.

In this procedure you will set the "Object Pooling" and "Object Construction" properties for Bank.Account.VC and Bank.UpadateReceipt.VC:

  1. Start the Component Services Explorer. Click Start, point to Programs, point to Administrative Tools, and then click Component Services.
  2. Select Console Root, click Component Services, click Computers, click My Computer, click COM+ Applications, click OPBank , and then click Components.
  3. Right-click on the Bank.Account.VC and then click Properties.
  4. Click the Activation tab.
  5. Click the Enable Object Pooling check box.
  6. Enter the minimum and maximum pool sizes-for example, 1 (min) and 10 (max). The Creation Timeout can remain at its default.
  7. Click the Enable Object Construction check box.
  8. For the Constructor string, type FILEDSN=OPBankSamples;DATABASE=pubs;UID=sa;PWD=;APP=OPBank
  9. Click OK.
  10. Repeat the procedure for the Bank.UpdateReceipt.VC component.
