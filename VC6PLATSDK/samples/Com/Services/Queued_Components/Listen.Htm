Starting a Listener For a Queued Application

  1. Start Component Services Explorer by clicking Start, point to Programs, point to Administrative Tools and then click Component Services.
  2. Select Console Root, click Component Services, click Computers, click My Computer, and then click COM+ Applications.
  3. Click a queued application (such as QCShip).
  4. Right-click the application and click Properties.
  5. Click the Queuing tab.
  6. Click Listen and then click OK.
  7. Right-click the application's icon again and then click Start.
